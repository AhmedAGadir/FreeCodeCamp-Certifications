I'm obsessed with birds, addicted to coffee and my need for adventure is always leading me to learn new skills and try new foods. I first started coding in September 2017 with freeCodeCamp and have been improving my craft since. The purpose of this page to tell you a bit about who I am, what I do and to show off some of my programming skills.

Education

I graduated with a bachelors in Mechanical Engineering from Queen Mary University of London in 2017. The mech eng program consistently developed my problem solving skills and cultivated in me the ability to learn new things quickly.


If you're wondering why I'm making the jump from engineering to tech, its simple. I thrive in positions where I can blend logical thinking with creative design - so a career in web development is a natural follow up.
